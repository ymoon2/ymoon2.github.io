My Creative Writing

As of 2019, I must say that I have not been in this major/field for a long time. I'm currently striving to learn all I can in such a short period of time, but time is quite unforgiving.

If there is one thing I learned from this experience, it's that you have to be resilient. I can say with confidence that I think I can keep going. And to you, my dear stranger/friend who is reading this, don't give up on your dreams so easily either. When life gives you lemons, eat them; no sour taste will best you!
